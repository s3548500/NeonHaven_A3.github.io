Career Plan

Compare and contrast the career plans, including ideal jobs, for each person in the group

There are 6 members in our group, each member has his own ideal job. There are some differences and also similarities between the jobs.The ideal jobs include Software developer, IT manager, Software engineer, Software builder, Digital product manager, IT engineer and programmer. Kelvin Tran’s ideal job is to be a software developer. The main task of this job is to identify, design, install and test the software systems they build for the company from scratch. The ideal job of Alex Strates is IT manager. IT managers are responsible for the coordination, planning and leadership of computer-related activities in the organization. They help to identify the organization’s IT needs and are responsible for implementing computer systems to meet the organization’s information systems requirements. Kemal Dogru wants to be a software engineer. People who do this job are senior IT personnel who design, install, and maintain a company’s computer systems. They are responsible for testing, configuring, and troubleshooting hardware, software, and network systems to meet the needs of the employing organization. Jiyuan Wu’s ideal job is software builder. The role of this job is to identify, design, install and test the software systems they build for the company from scratch. Qiurui Xu’s ideal job is digital product manager. This role is responsible for overseeing the entire product lifecycle, from defining the technology, and creating a great customer experience to winning customers. Yicheng Lu’s ideal jobs are IT engineer and programmer.IT engineers are senior IT personnel who design, install, and maintain a company’s computer systems. They are responsible for testing, configuring, and troubleshooting hardware, software and network systems to meet the needs of the employing organizations.

What common elements are there, if any?

There are many common elements among all the ideal jobs of our team members. All the ideal jobs require high level programming skills. Also all these jobs are required to meet the needs of the market. Some common practical skills are also needed, like problem solving skills and teamwork.

What differentiates each position from the others, if anything?

The different parts are mainly the tasks that are needed to be completed in each place. Some of the jobs are mainly to maintain the systems while some other task is to build new systems.

How similar or different are your career plans across the group?

To draw a conclusion, the ideal jobs are more similar rather than different. All the jobs are in the same field-- IT. Though jobs differ, they are all narrowed in the IT field. That is exactly why the ideal jobs look similar to each other.
